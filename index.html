For Alliance Leaders

How to share

Hand a single link to your kingdom and let everyone find the rest from the cross-link strip at the bottom of each guide.

Step 1

Send the codex link

Drop texnottexas.github.io/kingshot-codex into chat. Newcomers land here, see every guide at a glance, and pick what they need.

Step 2

Pin per topic

For specific questions, deep-link straight to the guide that answers it - the gear codex for forging, the garrison codex for defense, and so on.

Step 3

Plan with the tool

Before bear hunts, screenshot the rally-capacity tool with your leaders' numbers so joiners know exactly what to march with.

Step 4

Re-share

Every guide has a Google Translate flag picker in 18 languages and a mobile-friendly layout. Safe to forward alliance-wide or to a friend in another kingdom.
